Guidelines

The LFF supports 501c3 organizations that have been invited to apply for a grant. At this time we do not accept unsolicited proposals.

The Foundation reviews proposals on a quarterly basis. In general, the Foundation supports one-year grants. However, multi-year grants may be considered for organizations that have received prior Foundation support.
