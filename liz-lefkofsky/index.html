Liz Lefkofsky is the Director of the Lefkofsky Family Foundation which works to advance high impact programs, initiatives and research to enhance the quality of human life. Established in 2006 by Liz and her husband Eric Lefkofsky, a leading entrepreneur, the Foundation focuses on education, human rights, medical research and the arts. As a champion of public education, Liz has created a unique partnership between the Chicago Public Schools, the University of Chicago Urban Education Institute and the Academy for Urban School Leadership, to launch the Success Project in 38 middle schools in Chicago.
