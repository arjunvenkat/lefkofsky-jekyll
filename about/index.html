About LFF

The Lefkofsky Family Foundation is a private charitable foundation that was established in 2006 by Liz and Eric Lefkofsky.

The purpose of the Lefkofsky Family Foundation is to advance high-impact programs, initiatives and research that enhance the quality of human life in the communities we serve.

To achieve this mission, the Foundation strives to:

  • Ensure access to quality education
  • Improve fundamental human rights
  • Propel innovative medical research
  • Expand cultural initiatives
