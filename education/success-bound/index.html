Research shows that attendance and grades are critical factors in preparing students for success. Success Bound addresses these core factors along with supporting the development of important skills such as self-advocacy, time management, and vision setting. Through Success Bound school staff is equipped with FREE resources enabling them to set students on a path for success in high school, college, and beyond. Success Bound reaches more than 15,000 students through implementation in 100 schools across the city this year." ---
