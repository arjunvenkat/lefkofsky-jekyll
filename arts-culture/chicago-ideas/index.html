Their banner event brings together incredible world-class speakers in Chicago for one week in October to enlighten the audience around compelling ideas. In 2013 the LFF supported the establishment of the Chicago Ideas YOU(th) program, which each year invites 500 Chicago high school students to connect and engaged with Chicago Ideas speakers, influential businesses, their peers and the broader community. Students are empowered to seek new opportunities, make new connections and pursue their passions." ---
